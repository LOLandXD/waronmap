Buildings & Gold System Ideas for WarOnMap
============================================

BUILDINGS (built on owned nodes, cost gold + army + time)
----------------------------------------------------------

Barracks
  - Node produces +2 army/tick instead of +1 (or a flat multiplier).

Fort / Bunker
  - Defending army takes reduced losses; attackers must overcome a defense bonus.

Factory
  - Generates a small amount of gold per tick.

Mine
  - Generates more gold but consumes some army upkeep each tick.

Watchtower
  - Reveals adjacent enemy nodes, including their exact army count and owner.

Supply Depot
  - Transfers FROM this node send more troops per tick.

Road Hub
  - Connections TO this node move troops faster (lower travel time).

Wall
  - Enemy attacks against this node have reduced flow until the wall is breached.

Bank / Vault
  - Raises empire gold cap and protects some gold from capture.

Capitol
  - Unique per player; gives an empire-wide buff (e.g., +10% gold or +1 army/tick to all nodes).

Airfield / Harbor
  - Lets you launch one long-range attack per tick to a non-adjacent node.

Research Lab
  - Unlocks tech tiers over time (faster ticks, cheaper buildings, stronger forts).


GOLD SYSTEM IDEAS
-----------------

Passive Tax
  - Every owned node generates a baseline gold per tick; higher-degree / city nodes give more.

Capture Bounty
  - Taking a node gives a one-time gold reward; capturing from a player gives extra.

Loot
  - When you capture an enemy node, steal a percentage of their gold stockpile.

Building Cost
  - Buildings cost gold plus a small army investment and take several ticks to finish.

Reinforcement Rush
  - Spend gold to instantly add armies to a node during a defense.

Gold Cap
  - Each node can only hold so much gold, encouraging you to build banks or expand.

Trade Routes
  - A continuous connection between two of your cities generates bonus gold per tick.

Upkeep
  - Powerful buildings consume gold each tick; if you go broke, they deactivate.

Market / Trader
  - Spend gold to temporarily boost transfer rate or buy a neutral-node claim.

Prestige Reset
  - Late-game, spend a large pile of gold to prestige, gaining a permanent empire bonus and reshuffling the map.
