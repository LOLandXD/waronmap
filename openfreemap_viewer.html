Welcome to War on Map
This is a persistent multiplayer strategy game played on real road intersections. Register or log in to claim your starting territory.
Find your nodes
After logging in, the map will center on your green nodes. Each node generates +1 army every few seconds, up to a cap of 1 million.
Make a connection
Hold Space and drag from one of your green nodes to an adjacent node. A dotted line will follow your cursor; release on the target to open a road connection that moves troops every tick.
Adjust your army flow
Use the bottom-right panel to change how many troops to send per tick. Higher rates capture or reinforce faster.
